What I learnt in 6 months of Personal Training.

So, in the month of july of the year 2021 my height was almost 6ft and my
weight was 114 kgs.I was in the obese category and was feeling very
demotivated but, then I thought to join gym with my brother who was
already at a gym. So, I took up personal training and started my journey
of gymming and fitness.

Progress till now

At start I was unable to bench blank rod of 20 kgs so my trainer had to
specially put the smaller rod of 10 kgs which barely fit in bench press machine
for me. I was unable to dead 50kgs and I started with body weight squats only.

Exercises I did in the first week

  • Body weight Squats
  • Hyperextension
  • Front Raises
  • Leg raises
  • Rear Delt Fly
  • Cardio
    1. Cross
    2. Treadmill
    3. Cycling
